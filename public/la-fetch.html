What You Get

Eight systems, one platform.

Not eight separate tools bolted together — one console that owns the whole order lifecycle.

01

Order Flow Engine

Auto vendor-split orders moved through your defined stage pipeline (New → Confirmed → Ready → Dispatched → Delivered); COD, prepaid & partial-payment logic built in.

02

Operations Console

One screen for every order & vendor — vendor-wise fulfilment view, bulk actions, manual overrides, role-based access.

03

Custom Email Automation

Branded emails for every event: confirmed, dispatched, delayed, delivered, settled, penalty, out-of-stock, returns.

04

Dispatch Penalty Engine

Configurable dispatch-window per vendor, auto warning → penalty escalation, full audit log for disputes.

05

Easy Settlements

Auto commission & payable calculation, one-click move-to-payable, full settlement history — no manual push.

06

Real-Time Analytics

Live dispatch/delivery/RTO rate, vendor performance leaderboard, revenue & payment-mode breakdown.

07

Auto Order Tracking

Courier API integration, live tracking page for customers, out-for-delivery & delay alerts — zero manual AWB entry.

08

Returns & Exchanges

Fully automated request → approval → pickup → resolution — your team doesn't need to know it happened until it's done.
